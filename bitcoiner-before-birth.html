The Vision

What if your child could start
stacking sats from day zero?

Most children grow up with zero financial education and no savings built for them. Traditional savings lose to inflation. The future job market is uncertain. We're changing that — with Bitcoin.

⚡

Lightning Wallet at Birth

A non-custodial Lightning wallet created for your child, managed by the parent, ready to receive sats from day zero.

🌍

Global Community Support

The Bitcoin community worldwide can contribute sats that compound over a lifetime. Small donations today, life-changing wealth tomorrow.

🎮

Gamified Education

As the child grows, they earn sats by completing age-appropriate tasks — learning responsibility, discipline, and financial sovereignty.

🔐

Self-Custody at 18

When they turn 18, the child inherits a wallet built by community love and their own effort. True financial sovereignty begins.

The Journey

Campaign Roadmap

From the first ultrasound to the first self-custodial transfer.

Pre-Launch

Build the Community

Share the story on Bitcoin Twitter, Nostr, and Telegram. Create teasers with ultrasound + Lightning QR overlay. Get 21+ Geyser Launchpad followers.

Launch

Go Live on Geyser

Publish the campaign. Announce on all channels. Share the first ultrasound as the inaugural update. Ask early supporters to be the first contributors.

Birth Day

The Genesis Block

Announce the birth. Share the first photo. This is the "genesis block" of your child's Bitcoin journey. Expect a wave of community support.

Year 1–5

Milestones & First Tasks

Document milestones (first smile, first steps, first word). Introduce guided tasks. Post regular updates showing the growing wallet balance.

Year 5–18

The Long Stack

Task complexity grows with the child. They begin understanding Bitcoin, running a node, teaching others. The wallet grows steadily.

Year 18

Self-Custody Transfer

The ultimate milestone. Full ownership transfer. Your child becomes a sovereign Bitcoiner — with a wallet built by love, community, and effort.
